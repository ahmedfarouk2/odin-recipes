Have you ever wanted a Cheese Cake with no Cheese? Uh no? Well.. I still got it for you!!

Ingredients

  • Graham Crumbs
  • Greek Yoghurt
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk

Preparation

  1. Put graham crumbs into a microwave-safe pan and press them in

  2. Mix the yoghurt and sweetened condensed milk together

  3. Pour the mixture into the pan on top of the graham crumbs and microwave it

  4. Take it out after the cooking time and let it chill overnight
